The App

What is Mini Plan

Mini Plan is a comprehensive planning and task management application designed to help students and professionals organize their schedules, manage their workload, and track their progress toward their goals. With an intuitive interface and powerful features, Mini Plan makes it easy to stay on top of your responsibilities.

Impact

Stats & Results

Built with React Native and Firebase, Mini Plan has been piloted with over 100 students, resulting in improved productivity and 35% better task completion rates. Users report increased organization and reduced stress about managing their schedules.
