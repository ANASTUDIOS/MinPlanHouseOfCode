📅 The App

MinPlan

MinPlan is a minimalist college planner application to help students better keep track of important deadlines, scores, events, and programs so that they are better equipped to apply to college when the time comes. It uses an iterative process that allows students to quickly add or edit key areas of interest in an organized manner so that compiling important college application items is simple and easy. To keep everything clean and organized, the app primarily uses a system of drop down menus that allows the users to add items of interest like the descriptions of awards, community service events, or scholarship links without a hassle. The app also allows people to track events and upcoming tasks that they need to complete. In the future, I want to add a notification system to make the process even more seamless for users.
